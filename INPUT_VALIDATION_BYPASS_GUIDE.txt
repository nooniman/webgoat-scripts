====================================
INPUT VALIDATION BYPASS CHALLENGE
====================================

CHALLENGE: Send values that DON'T match the regex patterns

IMPORTANT: You need to VIOLATE the patterns, not match them!

====================================
REGEX PATTERNS TO VIOLATE:
====================================

Field 1: ^[a-z]{3}$
Valid:   abc (exactly 3 lowercase)
Invalid: ABC, 123, abcd, ab, a1b, ABC123 ✓

Field 2: ^[0-9]{3}$
Valid:   123 (exactly 3 digits)
Invalid: abc, 12, 1234, 1a2, ABC123 ✓

Field 3: ^[a-zA-Z0-9 ]*$
Valid:   abc 123 ABC (letters, numbers, spaces only)
Invalid: abc!, test@, #hash, special$ ✓

Field 4: ^(one|two|three|four|five|six|seven|eight|nine)$
Valid:   seven (word for digit 1-9)
Invalid: ten, zero, 7, SEVEN, One ✓

Field 5: ^\d{5}$
Valid:   01101 (exactly 5 digits)
Invalid: 1234, 123456, abcde, 12-34 ✓

Field 6: ^\d{5}(-\d{4})?$
Valid:   90210-1111 or 90210 (zip code)
Invalid: 1234, abc-1234, 90210-111 ✓

Field 7: ^[2-9]\d{2}-?\d{3}-?\d{4}$
Valid:   301-604-4882 (US phone)
Invalid: 101-234-5678 (starts with 1), abc-def-ghij ✓

====================================
ATTACK STRATEGY
====================================

Send values that:
1. Wrong format (letters instead of numbers)
2. Wrong length (too short or too long)
3. Special characters (!, @, #, $)
4. Wrong case (uppercase instead of lowercase)
5. Values outside enumeration

====================================
RECOMMENDED PAYLOADS:
====================================

Field 1 (should be 3 lowercase):
- ABC (uppercase)
- 123 (numbers)
- abcd (too long)
- ab (too short)
- a1b (mixed)
→ USE: ABC or 1234 or abcd

Field 2 (should be 3 digits):
- abc (letters)
- 12 (too short)
- 1234 (too long)
- 1a2 (mixed)
→ USE: abc or 1234 or 12

Field 3 (should be alphanumeric + space):
- abc! (exclamation)
- test@ (at sign)
- #hash (hashtag)
- special$ (dollar)
→ USE: test! or abc@test or #special

Field 4 (should be one/two/three...nine):
- ten (not in list)
- zero (not in list)
- 7 (number instead of word)
- SEVEN (uppercase)
→ USE: ten or zero or 123

Field 5 (should be 5 digits):
- 1234 (too short)
- 123456 (too long)
- abcde (letters)
- 12-34 (special char)
→ USE: 1234 or 123456 or abcde

Field 6 (should be 12345 or 12345-6789):
- 1234 (too short)
- abc-1234 (letters)
- 90210-111 (wrong dash format)
→ USE: 1234 or 123456789 or abc-1234

Field 7 (should be 201-234-5678):
- 101-234-5678 (starts with 1)
- abc-def-ghij (letters)
- 123456789 (no dashes, wrong format)
→ USE: 101-234-5678 or abc-def-ghij

====================================
METHOD 1: BROWSER CONSOLE (EASIEST)
====================================

1. Open lesson page
2. Press F12 → Console
3. Run this JavaScript:

// Bypass all validation
document.querySelector('input[name="field1"]').value = 'ABC';
document.querySelector('input[name="field2"]').value = 'abc';
document.querySelector('input[name="field3"]').value = 'test!@#';
document.querySelector('input[name="field4"]').value = 'ten';
document.querySelector('input[name="field5"]').value = '1234';
document.querySelector('input[name="field6"]').value = '123456789';
document.querySelector('input[name="field7"]').value = '101-234-5678';

// Submit
document.querySelector('form').submit();
